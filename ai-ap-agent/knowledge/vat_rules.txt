UK VAT VALIDATION RULES

1. VAT Number Requirement
- UK suppliers charging VAT must provide a valid VAT registration number.
- Invoices missing VAT numbers must be flagged.

2. VAT Calculation Check
- Standard VAT rate is 20 percent unless specified otherwise.
- VAT amount must match line item totals.

3. Zero Rated or Exempt Items
- Some goods and services may be VAT exempt or zero-rated.
- Such invoices must include the correct VAT code.

4. Compliance Failure Action
- If VAT validation fails, the invoice must not be paid.
- The supplier must be contacted for a corrected invoice.
