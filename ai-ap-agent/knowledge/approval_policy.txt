ACCOUNTS PAYABLE APPROVAL POLICY

1. Invoice Approval Thresholds
- Invoices up to £1,000 can be auto-approved by the system.
- Invoices between £1,000 and £10,000 require Manager approval.
- Invoices above £10,000 require CFO approval.

2. Missing Purchase Order (Non-PO Invoice)
- Any invoice received without a valid PO must be escalated.
- The requester must confirm the business purpose before payment.

3. Urgent Payments
- If an invoice is due within 48 hours, the system must notify the approver.
- Escalation is required if no response within SLA.

4. Segregation of Duties
- The same entity must not both approve and release payment.
- Payment execution must happen only after approval is logged.
