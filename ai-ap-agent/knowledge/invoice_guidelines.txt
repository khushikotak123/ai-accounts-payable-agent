INVOICE PROCESSING GUIDELINES

An invoice should contain:

- Supplier name and address
- Invoice number and date
- Amount due and currency
- VAT amount and VAT number if applicable
- Purchase Order reference if required
- Bank details (not editable without verification)

Processing Rules:

- Invoice must be recorded in the system before payment.
- Three-way match is required when PO and goods receipt exist.
- Exceptions must be routed to the appropriate workflow.
- Every action taken must be logged for audit purposes.
